Two Children From Same Parents Crushed To Death By Reckless Driver In Ogun

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  388 words  |  Lourie Helzer

Two children from the same parents have been killed by a reckless driver In Ogun state.

The children Olamide and Damilola Opayemi aged 15 and 11 respectively lost their lives on January 7. The driver, Moshudi Badmus Al-hassan, who was driving a Lexus also killed a third person while the elder brother of the deceased Opayemi narrowly escaped death.

An uncle of the Opayemis took to his Facebook account to mourn the loss of the children.
