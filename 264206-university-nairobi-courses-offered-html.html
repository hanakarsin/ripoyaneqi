University of Nairobi Courses Offered

 Posted on February 27, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1225 words  |  Enda Jantzen

University of Nairobi is a vibrant academic and Research Centre serving the larger East African community among other international students. The varsity offers an array of courses to its students at an affordable fee. Prospective students can now access details of various courses offered at University of Nairobi together with departmental contacts. UoN mainly offers PhD, masters, degree, diploma, certificate courses backed with high end facilities and laboratories. Applicants are required to fill out a designated application form, fetch the admission letter, register for courses, and pay fees during the intake process. Found in the heart of the capital city of Kenya, Nairobi UoN was first established in 1956 and later gained status as an independent institution in 1970.
