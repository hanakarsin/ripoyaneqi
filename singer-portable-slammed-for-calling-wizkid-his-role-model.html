Singer Portable Slammed For Calling Wizkid His Role Model

 Posted on March 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  176 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Nigerians have slammed singer Habeeb Okikiola popularly known as Portable for calling award–winning singer Wizkid his role model.

The Zazuu cronner revealed via a Twitter post that Wizkids song Ojuelegba was the reason he ventured into the music industry.

He tweeted: ”Wizkid Ojuelegba inspired me to start doing music.”

However, Nigerians have questioned if Wizkid song was truly the reason he went into music as there is no similarity between the way he and Wizkid sings.

Recall Portable recently apologized to Kenyas over the video where he told a lady he was not in for hookup.

The lady had walked out on Portable in a video he shared on his Instagram account on Sunday after he told her he wasn’t available for ‘hook up’.

Following the video, Kenyan media personality, Maureen Imbai, popularly known as Black Cinderella, threatened to sue the singer.
