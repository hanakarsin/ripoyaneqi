- They later took drums and sung in celebrations following the swearing-in of Uhuru at Kasarani on Tuesday,November 28

- Similar kinds of celebrations and streets funfair also took place in Nairobi's downtown Nyamakima and in Dagoretti

President Uhuru Kenyatta's inauguration threw his supporters into jovial moods,if sights and sounds from different regions over the same are anything to go by.

Jubilee Supporters in Murang'a town were not an exception as they joined their fellow supporters in celebrating Uhuru Kenyatta's swearing-in.
