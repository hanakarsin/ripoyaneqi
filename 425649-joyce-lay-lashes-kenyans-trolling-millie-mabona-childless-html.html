Joyce Lay Lashes Out at Kenyans Trolling Millie Mabona for Being Childless

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  390 words  |  Lourie Helzer
  • Joyce Lay told off Millie's critics holding that they should be ashamed of themselves
  • She encouraged Millie and assured her that God will definitely bless her in due time
  • Haters have been attacking Millie online claiming she might lose her marriage because she is barren

Former Taita Taveta women representative Joyce Lay has joined a number of people who have been standing with and supporting politician Millie Mabona following attacks from critics.

For some time now, several people have been attacking Millie with claims that she cannot sire children.

Some even went as far as noting that she could lose her man and the marriage for failing to give birth even at her age.

Well, irked by the attacks against Millie, Lay took to social media and told off her tormentors.

In a Facebook seen by TUKO.co.ke, she said people who have been attacking Millie should be ashamed of themselves for putting her through too much pain.

God's time is the best

Lay encouraged Millie by assuring her that she is more than a mother and that God will eventually bless her in due time.

"Shame on them who do that.You are planting a seed for your generation.You shall surely reap the same.I love you so much my sister Millie, and you are more than a mother!Hakuna kinachomshinda Mungu." Joyce Lay wrote.

Her post attracted the attention of her fans who camped on the comments section to agree with her.

Joyce Lay mourns mum

Lay's support for Millie comes about two months after the former politician lost her mother.

As earlier reported by TUKO.co.ke, Lay took to social media and shared two photos of her late mother followed by a touching caption as she broke the news to her followers.
