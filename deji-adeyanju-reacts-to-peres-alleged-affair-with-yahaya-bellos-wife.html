Deji Adeyanju Reacts To Pere's Alleged Affair With Yahaya Bello's Wife

 Posted on May 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  209 words  |  Chauncey Koziol

The convener of the Concerned Nigerian movement, Deji Adeyanju has reacted to the alleged affair between a former housemate in the Big Brother Naija, Pere Egbi and the wife of the Governor of Kogi State, Rashida Yahaya.

Recall that a communication expert, Jackson Ode had alleged that the reality star was in a romantic relationship with the Kogi State first lady.

Pere in his reaction to the accusation threatened to file a lawsuit against the journalist over the allegation levelled against him.

The BBNaija former housemate described the allegation as a demonic lie and a figment of a diseased imagination of Jackson Ode.

Pere wrote: “For the records, the publication about me and the First Lady of Kogi State is a demonic lie and a figment of a diseased imagination of Jackson Ode and those retweeting that rubbish, they must answer me in court both in Nigeria and in the United States of America where he is based.”

Adeyanju while reacting to the allegation levelled against the reality star and the wife of the Kogi State Governor said it is unacceptable.

The human rights activist wrote, “Spreading this about Yahaya Bello’s wife is unacceptable. I may not like Bello but we must never go low to this level.”
