What sets Cytonns The Alma apart from most residential properties?

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  700 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

The real estate sector in Kenya is increasingly becoming an attractive investment avenue for many, but not all the residential properties currently in the market are like The Alma, Cytonn's comprehensive residential development.

Thousands of investors are setting up flats across the country in efforts to tap into the opportunities available in the residential market.

And with buildings sprouting up all over the place, the customers are spoilt for choice.

However, because of the cutthroat competition, some developers have to trip over themselves to get buyers to notice their projects.

Most go the easy route and under-price their houses. The only problem with this is that in order to still make a profit, they have to cut corners during construction, and this often ends in disaster.

Remember the building that collapsed in Huruma?

Other developers take a more ethical path. Instead of compromising on quality, they distinguish themselves by offering features you wouldn't find anywhere else.

A great example of this is The Alma in Ruaka, a development by Cytonn Real Estate, which stands out from its competitors in many ways.

1. A parking bay

Land is expensive, we all know that. If you are a developer, you want to get as much use from your land as possible, even if it means placing your walls right up against the boundary. Usually, this means there is little space for parking.

Tenants end up having to leave their cars outside the property or look for alternative parking options, which can get expensive.

Residents of The Alma will not have this problem. Each house gets a designated spot at the parking bay (two, if you own a three-bedroom apartment).

Gone are the days of worrying about stolen side-mirrors, scratched bodywork or weather damage.

2. A retail centre

Convenience is everything nowadays. Discerning home-buyers know that, and they will appreciate the expansive retail centre at the face of The Alma.

Enough with commuting for hours just to get dinner or go shopping; everything is quite literally at the front door.

3. Back-up generators, 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance

Above everything else, a home should feel safe. The Alma caters for this with a monitored CCTV system, 24-hour a day guards, and backup generators.

Blackouts will be unheard of there, and homeowners will never need to worry about their security or that of their families.

4. Swimming pool and a gym

For the fitness enthusiasts out there, having a well equipped gym within the compound sounds heavenly.

Now, imagine having that and a swimming pool you can use whenever you please.

Picture weekends spent lounging poolside without having to leave your compound. It does not get much better than that, does it?

5. Elevators

Sure, taking the stairs is healthy, but when you live on the 10th floor, it is not always convenient.

Enter The Alma, with serviced elevators for the comfort of its tenants, and suddenly, your comings and goings are not so tedious anymore.

6. Elevated playgrounds

We all want our children to play outside in the sunshine and fresh air, but will they be safe doing it? Yes they will, if you live in The Alma.

The Alma's developers were certain to include well-equipped elevated playgrounds in the design, giving the young ones a chance to get some exercise as they learn to socialise with their age-mates.

More importantly, they do so in a controlled environment, away from strangers, unsafe roads and any other dangers from which they should be shielded.
