Is Daniel Sharman Aka the Weeping Monk From 'Cursed' Dating Anyone?

 Posted on March 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  570 words  |  Almeda Bohannan

Daniel Sharman is playing The Weeping Monk on the King Arthur prequel series 'Cursed' on Netflix. Details on his personal life and his career.

While the final season of Game of Thrones was underwhelming for many viewers, there hasn't quite been a fantasy series that has managed to fill the void that the HBO series left behind. Netflix's Cursed aims to change that with violent, gory deaths and a leading lady with sword-wielding skills that could rival Arya Stark. The series is based on a graphic novel by Frank Miller and Tom Wheeler, and it's a prequel to the legend of King Arthur and the Excalibur sword.

But, Cursed is not a typical origin story as it centers around Nimue (Katherine Langford), the future Lady of the Lake, instead of Arthur himself. 

Teen Wolf and The Originals alum Daniel Sharman is starring in the series as the Weeping Monk, a mysterious villain-esque character with more than a few secrets to share. 

Despite playing a duplicitous figure on the show, the British actor has already stolen the hearts of some viewers. Does Daniel Sharman have a girlfriend? Keep reading to find out what else you've seen the actor in before and to learn more about his personal life.  

Does Daniel Sharman have a girlfriend?

The actor does not appear to be in a relationship at this time, but he has been linked to several famous faces in the past. He starred as Isaac Lahey on the MTV series Teen Wolf from 2012 to 2014, and he reportedly dated his co-star Crystal Reed during part of that time. After their split in 2013, Daniel and Crystal continued working together on the show and their respective characters dated throughout Season 3.  

Following her breakup with Australian singer Cody Simpson in 2014, Gigi Hadid was rumored to be seeing the British actor. The two were seen out and about in New York City in October of that year, but they never confirmed their connection.

In 2015, Daniel started dating model and TV presenter Asha Leo. The pair made several public appearances together, including one at a New Jersey Devils hockey game. The couple parted ways in the summer of that year. 

What has Daniel Sharman starred in?

The 34-year-old actor was born and raised in Hackney, a borough in London. He received his undergraduate degree from the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, and his big break was in the 2010 independent film The Last Days of Edgar Harding. 

The following year, Daniel appeared alongside Henry Cavill, Freida Pinto, and Luke Evans as the God Ares in the movie Immortals. He also acted in two episodes of the Freeform series The Nine Lives of Chloe King in 2011 too.

Daniel's first major regular role on TV was in Teen Wolf, and his character was present for 31 episodes of the young adult drama. In 2013, he starred in the film version of the hit Hallmark series When Calls the Heart as Edward. He also played the dual character of Kol Mikaelson, the youngest Mikaelson son, and Kaleb Westphall in Season 2 of The CW series The Originals. 

His other TV credits include the character of Troy Otto in Season 3 of Fear the Walking Dead and as Renaissance man Lorenzo de'Medici in the final two seasons of Medici: The Magnificent. 

Season 1 of Cursed is now available to stream on Netflix.
