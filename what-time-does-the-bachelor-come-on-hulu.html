Did You Miss 'The Bachelor'? Here's When You Can Stream It on Hulu

If you're waiting to stream episodes, don't wait too long. For many Hulu users, the latest 'Bachelor' episodes expire after a certain period of time.

By Callie (Carlos) Cadorniga

Published March 18 2024, 10:04 a.m. ET

With modern TV, there's now a certain invasive sense of urgency when it comes to watching the latest episodes. In the past, we had to tune into a channel every week to keep up with new installments of our favorite shows. These days, we have all sorts of outlets to watch new episodes at our own pace, which can often be a double-edged sword in a content-heavy market. The best thing about it, though, is the ability to watch new episodes on our own time.

Instant spoiler discussions aside, we're no longer tied down by the need to watch new episodes right as they come out on TV as long as we have a stable internet connection. So shows like The Bachelor are more available at our fingertips than ever before. All of the romance and roses from previous seasons and current ones can be watched whenever we want. Well, almost whenever.

New episodes of The Bachelor are available on Hulu every week, but when exactly do they come out?

Here's when you can watch new episodes of 'The Bachelor' on Hulu.

Despite all the options we have to watch the latest TV episodes on streaming platforms like Hulu, networks have often tried to strike a balance between broadcast and streaming. After all, channels such as ABC still want you to catch their newest shows on the actual channel to justify ratings and time slots. In essence, they still treat both TV ratings and streaming numbers with the same amount of gravity and they want to make sure that the channel itself still performs well compared to streaming.

It's for this reason that more often than not, networks will still air their newest episodes on regular TV and only make streaming available shortly after. For primetime shows like The Bachelor, you typically only have to wait until the morning after to watch. However, this strategy maintains the urgency of watching something right away to avoid details getting spoiled.

If you absolutely can't watch The Bachelor when it airs, then the latest episodes will always appear on Hulu at 5 a.m EST on the morning after they've already aired on ABC. If you missed the usual broadcast, you can easily squeeze in a new episode in the wee hours before going off to work or classes. But even if you're waiting for them to stream, you may not want to wait long. For many Hulu users, the latest Bachelor episodes will expire after a certain period of time.

Be sure to know when and where you can watch The Bachelor so you don't miss a second of the drama and romance. New episodes premiere every Monday at 9 p.m. EST on ABC.
