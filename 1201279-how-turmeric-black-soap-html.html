How to make African black soap with turmeric powder at home?

Here is the recipe of black soap with turmeric from scratch:

  • We start with drying the plantain skins, you can dry them in the oven or under the sun. When the skins are dried, it is time to burn them to receive the ash, it is a very important ingredient of this type of soap;
  • Take the aches and dissolve it in warm distilled water to receive a dark solute;
  • Put the pot with the solute on fire, and bring it to boil;
  • After a few minutes of boiling, pour the mixture through a sieve. See also: How to make black soap at home
  • Now it is time to heat up the base oil, you can use cocoa butter, shea butter or palm kernel oil;
  • When the base oil or butter is melted, add the ash to liquid and stir it well. At this step, it is high time to add one teaspoon of turmeric powder to the mixture. If you want to add some other additional ingredients, for example, dried herbs or essential oils, you can do it at this step;
  • Keep heating the mixture, there should appear a dark, frothy substance on the surface, and it is actually the black soap;

There is also one more method of turmeric black soap preparation. This method is much easier because you can use already bought or previously made black soap. In this method, you just customize the soap with necessary ingredients. Here are the steps to add the turmeric powder into your soap:

  • We can start with the preparation of the moulds by lubricating them with coconut oil;
  • The next step is to chop the piece of black soap;
  • It is time to make a double boiler, just place a pot with water on the stove, take a smaller bowl and put it into the water;
  • Put the pieces of black soap into the bowl and melt them, don’t forget to stir them regularly, to prevent overheating or burning the soap;
  • Turn off the heat and add our special ingredient - turmeric, one tablespoon of powder is enough for every soap bar;
