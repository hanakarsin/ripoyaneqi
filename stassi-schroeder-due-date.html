Former Vanderpump Rules Star Stassi Schroeder Is Pregnant Again When Is Her Due Date?

 Posted on February 28, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  780 words  |  Hettie Henneman

The former 'Vanderpump Rules' star welcomed her first child with Beau Clark in January 2021. Now that she's pregnant again, fans are dying to know: When is Stassi Schroeder due?

Vanderpump Rules cast member Stassi Schroeder has been a central character since the popular series began airing in 2013 — until June 2020, that is. As it turns out, her past racially insensitive comments came back to haunt her and fellow cast member Kristen Doute — prompting Bravo to cut ties with them.

The network also fired Max Boyens and Brett Caprioni following the resurfacing of past comments many have deemed racist. While the firing shocked VPR’s fans, Stassi focused on expanding her family with her husband, Beau Clark.

Stassi and Beau welcomed their first child, Hartford Charlie Rose, in January 2021. In 2023, the former Vanderpump Rules star announced she is pregnant for the second time! So, when is Stassi Schroeder’s due date? Keep scrolling to find out!

Stassi Schroder hid her second pregnancy from fans until March 2023.

Us Weekly exclusively reported that Stassi was pregnant and expecting her first child with Beau in June 2020. The pregnancy news came after they admitted to practicing making a baby throughout the COVID-19 quarantine.

At the time, Stassi was spotted with Beau as they picked up food in Los Angeles, and she was reportedly rocking a long cardigan that "covered her baby bump," the publication reported. Stassi later confirmed her pregnancy by announcing the sex of her baby via Instagram. "We're having a baby girl," she captioned a sweet photo published on June 23, 2020.

Stassi said in February 2020 that she and Beau routinely discussed having a baby before they became engaged. On January 7, 2021, the reality TV alum welcomed Hartford three days after her due date.

Since giving birth, Stassi found new success as a New York Times Bestselling author for her books Off With My Head and Next Level Basic. She and Beau also created a podcast, The Good, the Bad, and the Baby. In March 2023, the couple posted a reel on their podcast’s page to make a surprising announcement — they’re expecting again!

“We had to come here first because we had to tell y’all first,” Stassi told her fans, followed by Beau sharing baby no. 2’s sonogram. Stassi also posted an official picture of her holding her baby bump next to Hartford.

“Secrets stress me out,” she captioned the photo on Instagram. Baby #2, I love you so much already.”

Stassi and Beau said on social media that they conceived the baby during their trip to Vegas in December 2022. While she hasn’t disclosed her due date yet, based on the math, their second baby should arrive in September or October 2023.

When did Stassi and Beau get married?

Except in a surprise twist, Stassi and Beau ended up tying the knot anyway in October 2020! On Oct. 7, she shared a video clip of their September wedding, writing, "Today would’ve been our wedding day. We went and did it anyway ... Hopefully, Italian dream wedding oct 202 ... I am so proud to be your wife."

Then, in May 2022, Stassi and Beau finally had their dream wedding in Rome, Italy. During their intimate ceremony, Stassi wore an off-the-shoulder tulle gown by Galia Lahav in a custom “Magnolia Style” dress, per Page Six. Since making their romance legal, the couple has seemingly enjoyed collaborating personally and professionally.

Stassi's pregnancy news came days after she was fired from 'Vanderpump Rules.'

Bravo announced it cut ties with Stassi — along with Kristen, Max, and Brett — on June 9, 2020, following the resurfacing of past comments many have deemed racist. Stassi and Kristen had called the police on former SURver Faith Stowers for a crime she did not commit in 2018.

“Racially insensitive comments from my past have resurfaced,” Stassi wrote on Instagram on June 7. “I am grateful for the people in my life that continue to check me and push me to evolve into a more educated person ... What I did to Faith was wrong. I apologize and I do not expect forgiveness.”

Since Stassi's exit, she hasn't shared any plans to return to reality TV anytime soon.

Vanderpump Rules airs Wednesdays on Bravo at 9 p.m. EST.
