Babymama Slams Funke Akindele's Husband For 'Teaching' 17-year-old Son How To Smoke

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  184 words  |  Hettie Henneman

Mella, the former partner of JJC Skillz, has once again alleged that her son Benito learnt how to smoke from his father.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that JJC Skillz, who is married to Funke Akindele, had come under heavy criticism after Mella proudly showed off their 17-year-old son smoking.

A few months ago, Mella opened up on how JJC Skillz’s smoking habit had a great influence on the life of their son.

According to the embittered babymama, Funke Akindele’s husband is a drug addict and his mother had expressed worry over his consistent smoking habits in the presence of his children.

Reacting once again to a picture of Benito smoking, she stated in an already deleted post that Skillz is responsible for her son’s habit.

She wrote: “The biggest problem here is that Abdul is a long time drug user who has constantly smoked dope in front of all his children which has been a long-standing dispute between us both.

“These children look up to their father and follow his example. It’s been a big problem for me and his mother.”
