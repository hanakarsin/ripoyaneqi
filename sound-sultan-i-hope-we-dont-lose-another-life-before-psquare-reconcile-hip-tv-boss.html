Sound Sultan: 'I Hope We Don't Lose Another Life Before PSquare Reconcile'

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  226 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Nigerian entertainment entrepreneur and television executive, Ayo Animashaun has urged defunct duo, Psquare, Peter and Paul Okoye, to bury their hatchet and make peace. 

This is coming after members of the defunct Platashun Boyz; 2face Idibia, Blackface and Faze, made peace at the Night of Tribute held in honour of singer Sound Sultan in Terra Kulture, Lagos state on Sunday, July 25 2021.

Naija News understands that the singers in honour of Sound Sultan who was their bosom friend and colleague made peace and settle their differences. 

 They were videoed performing their classic songs together which melted the hearts of the audience. 

Animashaun, via his Instagram, pointed out that he hopes it doesn’t take the death of another person for the brothers, Paul and Peter Okoye, to bury the hatchet.

He wrote; “My picture of the month….I hope we don’t lose another life before @iamkingrudy and @peterpsquare realise it’s their turn to bury the hatchet. @therealblackfacenaija @fazealone @official2baba”

Meanwhile, Nollywood actress, Empress Njamah has urged defunct P-Square, Peter and Paul Okoye to embrace love and peace whilst reflecting on late Sound Sultan’s demise.

Sharing a video from the late veteran singer’s tribute night, actress Empress pleaded with social media to stop comparing the two brothers and criticizing them as it’d only worsen the beef between them.

She also revealed that she misses them as a team.
