In 2018, Oliver cautioned Meghan to be wary about getting married in the royal family.

While appearing on The Late Night Show with Stephen Colbert, the comedian revealed to the host that he would not dare marry into the royal family and even advised Markle to pull out the last minute.

Colbert asked him if he was excited about the then-upcoming wedding between Prince Harry and Markle and he said:

''No. I would not blame her if she pulled out of this at the last minute. I do not think you need to have just seen the pilot episode of The Crown to get a basic sense she might marry into a family that could cause her so many emotional complications.''

The father of two also joked about how the royal family still practices things that seem odd for grownups in this generation.

''They are an emotionally stunted group of flawed people doing a silly pseudo job and that is what she is marrying into so I hope she likes it. It is going to be weird for her.
