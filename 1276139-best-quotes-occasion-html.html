Thank you God quotes

Many religions exist in the contemporary world. Despite the differences in doctrines and practices, they all direct their followers to a Supreme Deity who sustains the lives of all human beings. It is, therefore, essential to be grateful to this Supreme Being for everything that we have in life;

  • "O Lord, who lends me life, lend me a heart replete with thankfulness." - William Shakespeare.
  • “We need to discover all over again that worship is natural to the Christian, as it was to the godly Israelites who wrote the psalms, and that the habit of celebrating the greatness and graciousness of God yields an endless flow of gratefulness, joy, and zeal.” - J.I. Packer.
  • “What I have done is worthy of nothing but silence and forgetfulness, but what God has done for me is worthy of everlasting and thankful memory.” - Joseph Hall.
  • “There are three requisites to the proper enjoyment of earthly blessings: a grateful reflection, on the goodness of the giver; a deep sense of our own unworthiness; and a recollection of the uncertainty of our long possessing them. The first will make us grateful; the second, humble; and the third, moderate.” - Hannah More.
  • "A grateful heart is a beginning of greatness. It is an expression of humility. It is a foundation for the development of such virtues as prayer, faith, courage, contentment, happiness, love, and well-being." - James E. Faust.

  • "I am indebted to my father for living, but to my teacher for living well." - Alexander the Great.
  • "Whoever first coined the phrase 'you're the wind beneath my wings' most assuredly was reflecting on the sublime influence of a very special teacher." - Frank Trujillo.
  • "Your heart is slightly bigger than the average human heart, but that's because you're a teacher." - Aaron Bacall.
  • "One looks back with appreciation to the brilliant teachers, but with gratitude to those who touched our human feelings." - Carl Jung.

Thank you mom quotes

Mothers are amazing. They sacrifice a lot for their children to have the best lives. Usually, they make sacrifices without any complaint. They are the first friends and support system that we meet in life. Each day, you should celebrate and appreciate your mother.

  • "I love my mother as trees love water and sunshine. She helps me grow, prosper, and reach great heights." - Terry Guillemets.
  • "All that I am, or hope to be, I owe to my angel mother" - Abraham Lincoln.
  • "If I know what love is, it's because of you." - Hermann Hesse.
  • "My mother was the most beautiful woman I ever saw. All I am, I owe to my mother. I attribute my success in life to the moral, intellectual and physical education I received from her." - George Washington.
  • "Mother's love is bliss, is peace, it need not be acquired, it need not be deserved. If it is there, it is like a blessing; if it is not there, it is as if all the beauty had gone out of life." - Erich Fromm.

Importance of gratefulness

Being grateful is not something that should be taken lightly. It is important for the following reasons;

  • It improves our emotional health - Being grateful makes us happier people, decreases symptoms of depression and enhances our resilience.
  • It enhances our physical health: Human beings sleep better when they are grateful. Gratefulness also reduces high blood pressure, minimises stress and activates the body's healing relaxation response.
  • It enhances our social lives.
  • It leads to improved professional lives.

We all have something to be grateful for and people we appreciate in our lives. We hope that you find the perfect birthday thank you quotes or sayings for any other occasion from the above list. Remember to be grateful for everything and everyone you have in life. It is the top secret for establishing fruitful relationships with different people in your social and professional circles.
